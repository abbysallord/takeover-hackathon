# Takeover: AI-Powered Sales & Automation Workspace

**Takeover** is an intelligent workspace platform designed to act as your autonomous AI sales assistant. It monitors your inbox, understands your business offerings, identifies potential leads, and drafts quotes automatically—turning hours of manual email processing into minutes of simple approvals.

---

## 🚀 Core Value Proposition
Takeover eliminates the manual busywork of inbound sales. Instead of a human reading every email, manually extracting contact info, looking up pricing sheets, and drafting a quote from scratch, the **Takeover AI** does all the heavy lifting instantly. It only requires a human to click "Approve" before sending the final response.

---

## 🛠️ Key Features

### 1. Dynamic AI Knowledge Base
You can upload your company's unique **Product Catalogs** and **Pricing Sheets** directly into the workspace. The AI uses this data to learn exactly what you sell, your SKUs, your pricing tiers, and your business rules. Whenever an email comes in, the AI consults this knowledge base to craft accurate, context-aware responses.

### 2. Seamless Gmail Integration
By connecting your Gmail account securely via Google OAuth, Takeover actively monitors your inbox in the background. You don't need to forward emails or manually log them into a CRM; the system catches them as soon as they arrive.

### 3. Automated Lead Extraction
When a new email arrives, the AI reads it and determines if it's a potential customer. If it is, the AI automatically extracts the sender's Name, Email, and Company Name, and creates a **Customer Profile** and an active **Lead** in your dashboard.

### 4. Autonomous Quotation Drafting
If an incoming email asks for a price estimate or product inquiry, the AI doesn't just notify you—it takes action. It cross-references the email request with your uploaded pricing sheets, calculates the costs, and automatically drafts a professional **Quotation**.

### 5. Human-in-the-Loop Approvals
Takeover is fully autonomous but completely under your control. Before the AI sends any quotation or response back to a customer, the workflow is paused and flagged for **Approval**. You can review the AI's drafted email, check the generated quote, make any necessary adjustments, and hit "Approve."

### 6. Workflow & Automation Tracking
Every action the AI takes is recorded in a step-by-step **Workflow**. You can easily track the lifecycle of any interaction (e.g., *Email Received ➔ Lead Extracted ➔ Quote Drafted ➔ Pending Approval*).

### 7. Performance & ROI Analytics Dashboard
The dashboard gives you a bird's-eye view of how much time and money the AI is saving you. It tracks:
- **Revenue Pipeline**: The total monetary value of all active leads and quotes generated by the AI.
- **Time Saved**: An estimation of the manual hours saved by automating the drafting process.
- **AI Confidence**: The average confidence score of the AI's decisions, helping you trust its autonomy.
- **Workflow Stats**: Counters for emails received, workflows running, and pending approvals that need your attention.

---

## 🔄 The User Journey
1. **Onboarding**: You sign up, enter your company details, upload your catalog/pricing PDFs or text, and connect your Gmail account.
2. **Monitoring**: Takeover silently polls your inbox in the background.
3. **Action**: A customer emails you asking for a price on "100 Widgets."
4. **Automation**: Takeover reads the email, finds "Widgets" in your pricing sheet, calculates the total for 100 units, creates a Lead, and drafts a reply with a quote.
5. **Approval**: You receive a notification on your dashboard, review the draft, and click "Approve."
6. **Conversion**: The email is sent to the customer, and the pipeline value is updated on your dashboard.
