# Sales Discount & Approval Policies

This document governs the discounts sales representatives can apply and outlines the strict human-in-the-loop manager approval limits.

## 1. Standard Volume Discount Tiers
Sales reps and automated agents can automatically apply the following volume-based discounts:

*   **Widget A & Widget B**:
    *   Quantity >= 100 units: 10% discount.
    *   Quantity >= 500 units: 20% discount.
*   **Widget C**:
    *   Strict policy: No volume discounts allowed for orders below 50 units.
    *   Quantity >= 50 units: 5% discount (requires manual verification of stock).
*   **Server Rack**:
    *   Flat rate, no volume discount available. Free delivery applies.

## 2. Manager Approval Limits (HIPL Gate)
To safeguard transactions, a **Manager Approval** is strictly required under any of the following conditions:

*   **Order Value Gate**: Any single quotation with a total grand value exceeding **$3,000.00 USD** must be paused and sent to the manager for approval.
*   **Manual Discount Requests**: Any custom discounts requested by customers that do not conform to the predefined volume tiers must be approved by a manager.
*   **Stock Warnings**: Orders for Widget C or Server Racks when inventory is below safety thresholds require approval.
